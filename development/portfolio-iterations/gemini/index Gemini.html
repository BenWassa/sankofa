About Project Sankofa

Project Sankofa is an initiative dedicated to exploring the intersection of ancient wisdom, contemporary research, and speculative futures. Drawing inspiration from the Akan concept of Sankofa – 'go back and get it' – we aim to retrieve valuable knowledge from the past to inform and enrich our understanding of the present and shape a more conscious future. This portfolio serves as a central hub for our multi-faceted output, including podcasts, visual narratives, and strategic briefs.
